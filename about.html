The Mission


Eric is committed to delivering a distinct beauty experience to the discerning individual while encouraging healthy hair habits.

Then


Eric found his passion for hairstyling by doing his friends' hair in the kitchen of his small Berkeley apartment. Splashy indie rock reverberating from the stereo, the living room was packed with bffs talking trash and laughing while waiting their turn for a trim.

Now


Over the years, Eric's work has brought him from neighborhood barbershops to upscale salons. Educated in San Francisco, Los Angeles and New York, Eric has developed a mature talent resulting in a diverse clientele. He is currently stationed at Blow Salon in downtown Berkeley. Drop in and say hi, or book an appointment!
